How to Deposit

  • What can be deposited into the Repository?

    The Repository terms of use determine the types of content that can be deposited into Veritus eCommons.

    Research Output Eligibility

    Only Research Outputs connected to Uganda Christian University can be deposited in the Repository. By "connected" we mean:

    • Research Outputs authored by current or former UCU researchers, research students or staff members
    • Outputs of research conducted at Uganda Christian University
    • Research Outputs that appear in a journal published, or a conference hosted, by the University
    • Outputs resulting from research undertaken using University facilities

    Before Depositing

    Regardless of the output type, we ask you to check that:

    • You have the right to deposit the material
    • The material is available in suitable electronic format(s)

  • Open Access and Scholarly Outputs

  • Batch Upload Service

    For complex or extensive material, we offer a batch upload service.

    What You Need

    For a batch upload, we require:

    1. A spreadsheet containing metadata describing the items for upload. We will supply a template spreadsheet with the required format and fields.
    2. The files themselves in appropriate electronic formats.

    Process

    Once we receive the spreadsheet and files, our team will upload the material to the repository on your behalf and report back once Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) have been created.

    Get Started

    To obtain further advice and to get a copy of the template spreadsheet, please contact:

    Research Data: Research Data Management team - info@data.ucu.ac.ug

    Other Materials: Repository service - support@repository.ucu.ac.ug

    If you're unsure which applies, contact the Repository team and they'll route your enquiry appropriately.

  • Self-Archiving Your Material

    In some instances, you may be allowed to self-archive your material on the repository.

    How to Request Access

    Please contact support@repository.ucu.ac.ug with details of which collection(s) you wish to submit your material to.

    Approval Process

    Once the team has verified your access rights to deposit in that collection, they will send you an email letting you know that you can deposit your material.

    New Communities or Collections

    If you would like a new community and/or collection to be created, please email the Repository team with:

    • The name of the organisational entity (such as Department or Institution)
    • A description of which types of content the community/collection will contain (e.g., research publications, working papers, images)
