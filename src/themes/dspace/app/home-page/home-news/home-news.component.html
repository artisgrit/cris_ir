Institutional Repository: A Centre of Excellence in the Heart of Africa

Welcome to Uganda Christian University's Digital Institutional Repository. Our repository is dedicated to preserving and providing access to the scholarly, educational and cultural output of UCU. This platform showcases the intellectual contributions of our faculty, researchers, and students.

The UCU Institutional Repository supports our commitment to:

  • Promote open access to scholarly research and knowledge
  • Preserve the intellectual heritage of Uganda Christian University
  • Enhance the visibility and impact of UCU research globally
  • Support academic collaboration and knowledge sharing across disciplines
  • Ensure long-term preservation of digital scholarly materials

Explore our collections and discover the innovative research and scholarship emerging from Uganda Christian University.
